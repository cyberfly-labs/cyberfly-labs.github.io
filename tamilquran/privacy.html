Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

Tamil Quran Hadith Audio is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Tamil Quran Hadith Audio is an offline-first application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on our servers.

  • All text-to-speech audio is generated locally on your device using Facebook's MMS-TTS-TAM AI model.
  • We do not track your listening history or share any data with third parties.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: Required to store the TTS model and generated audio files.
  • Notifications: Optional, for playback controls.
