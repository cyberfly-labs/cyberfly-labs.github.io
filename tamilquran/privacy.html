Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

1. Overview

Tamil Quran Hadith Audio ("we", "our", or "us") is developed and maintained by Cyberfly Labs. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Tamil Quran Hadith Audio handles your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Tamil Quran Hadith Audio is an offline-first application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on our servers.

  • All text-to-speech audio is generated locally on your device using Facebook's MMS-TTS-TAM AI model.
  • No text, audio, or personal data is sent to any server or cloud service.
  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not track your listening history or share any data with third parties.

3. AI Model

This app uses Facebook's MMS-TTS-TAM (Massively Multilingual Speech — Text-to-Speech, Tamil) model to generate Tamil audio from text. The model runs entirely on your device and does not require internet connectivity. No text or audio data is transmitted externally during speech synthesis.

4. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to function:

  • Storage: Required to store the TTS model and generated audio files on your device.
  • Notifications: Optional, for playback controls in the notification area.

5. Third-Party Services

The app embeds Facebook's MMS-TTS-TAM model locally for offline speech synthesis. No data is sent to Facebook or any other third party during normal app usage. We do not sell or share personal data with any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and contains only Islamic educational content.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cyberflylabs.com.
