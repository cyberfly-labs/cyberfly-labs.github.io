Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

EdgeDox is built to process sensitive documents on your mobile device. We prioritize your privacy above all else.

1. Mobile Privacy First

Our fundamental philosophy is that your data belongs to you. EdgeDox operates entirely on-device, ensuring that your confidential documents are never exposed to external servers.

2. Data Processing

All document analysis, OCR, and data extraction happen locally on your smartphone using on-device AI models.

  • No Cloud Uploads: We do not upload your documents, images, or extracted text to any cloud server for processing.
  • Local Storage: Extracted data is stored only on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.
  • No Telemetry: We do not track what documents you scan or the contents of those documents.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions strictly for its core functionality:

  • Camera: To capture documents for intelligence processing.
  • Storage/Files: To import documents and save the extracted data locally.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this Privacy Policy.

5. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries or concerns, please contact us at support@cyberflylabs.com.
