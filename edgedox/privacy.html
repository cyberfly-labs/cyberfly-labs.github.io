Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. Mobile Privacy First

EdgeDox is built to process sensitive documents on your mobile device. We prioritize your privacy above all else.

2. Data Processing

All document analysis and data extraction happen locally on your smartphone using on-device AI models.

  • No Cloud Uploads: We do not upload your documents to any cloud server for processing.
  • Local Storage: Extracted data is stored only on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera/Storage: To capture or import documents for intelligence processing.

4. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact privacy@cyberflylabs.com.
