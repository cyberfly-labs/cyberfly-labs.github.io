Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

NoiseFix is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

NoiseFix is an offline-first application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data, audio files, or processed results on our servers.

  • All AI noise reduction processing happens locally on your device.
  • Your audio and video files never leave your phone.
  • We do not share any data with third parties since we do not collect it.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Microphone: Optional, for recording audio directly within the app.
  • Storage/Media Library: To import audio/video files for processing and save denoised results.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@cyberflylabs.com.
