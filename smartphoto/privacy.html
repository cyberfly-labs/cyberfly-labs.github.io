Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

1. Overview

SmartPhoto AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application SmartPhoto AI handles your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

SmartPhoto AI is an offline-first application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data, photos, or processed results on our servers.

  • All AI photo enhancement and style processing happens locally on your device.
  • Your photos never leave your phone.
  • We do not share any data with third parties since we do not collect it.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera: Optional, for taking photos directly within the app.
  • Storage/Media Library: To import photos for processing and save enhanced results.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cyberflylabs.com.
