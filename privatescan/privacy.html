Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

PrivateScan ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application PrivateScan handles your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

PrivateScan is an offline-first application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data, scanned documents, or OCR results on our servers.

  • All processing happens locally on your device.
  • We do not record your keystrokes or scanned text.
  • We do not share any data with third parties since we do not collect it.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera: To scan documents.
  • Storage/Gallery: To import images for scanning and save results.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cyberflylabs.com.
