Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Decentralization and Privacy

The Cyberfly Network is built on decentralized principles. Devices on the network communicate directly or through decentralized nodes.

2. Data Handling

  • Edge Inference: Raw data is processed on edge devices. It is not transmitted to central servers.
  • Metadata: Minimal metadata needed for network routing and consensus may be visible on the public ledger/network, but it is anonymized where possible.

3. Node Operators

If you operate a node, you are responsible for maintaining the security and privacy of the traffic you facilitate, in accordance with the network protocol.

4. Contact

For questions about the network protocol and privacy, contact privacy@cyberflylabs.com.
