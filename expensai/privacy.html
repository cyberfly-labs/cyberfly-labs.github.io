Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Overview

expensAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application expensAI handles your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

expensAI is a fully offline application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data, financial information, receipts, SMS content, or voice recordings on any external servers.

  • All voice recognition and natural language processing happens locally on your device.
  • All receipt scanning and OCR processing happens locally on your device.
  • All SMS parsing and transaction detection happens locally on your device.
  • All expense categorization and AI analysis happens locally on your device.
  • We do not record, upload, or share your financial data with any third party.
  • Your expense history, budgets, and financial insights are stored only on your device.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Microphone: To capture voice input for expense logging. Audio is processed on-device and never transmitted.
  • Camera: To scan receipts for automatic expense extraction. Photos are processed on-device and never uploaded.
  • SMS (Read Only): To detect bank transaction messages and auto-log expenses. SMS content is processed locally and never leaves your device.
  • Storage: To store AI models, expense data, and exported reports locally on your device.
  • Notifications: To send budget alerts and spending reminders locally.

4. On-Device AI Models

expensAI uses on-device AI models (ASR, OCR, NLP, and classification models) that run entirely on your phone. These models are downloaded once during initial setup and do not require an internet connection to function afterwards.

5. Financial Data Security

All financial data — including expense records, receipt images, and parsed SMS transactions — is encrypted and stored locally on your device. We have no ability to access, view, or recover your financial data.

6. SMS Data Handling

expensAI reads SMS messages solely to detect bank transaction notifications. The app only processes messages matching known bank/financial SMS patterns. No SMS content is stored beyond the extracted transaction data, and no messages are transmitted externally.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since all processing is on-device and no data is transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cyberflylabs.com.
