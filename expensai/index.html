Three Ways to Track Expenses

🎙️

Voice Input

Just say "Spent ₹250 on groceries at Spencer's" and expensAI will parse the amount, category, and merchant automatically using on-device speech recognition and NLP.

📸

Receipt Scanning

Point your camera at any receipt — expensAI uses on-device OCR and AI extraction to pull out the total, store name, date, and individual items. No photo leaves your phone.

💬

SMS Auto-Detect

Automatically detects bank transaction SMS and credit card alerts. Silently logs debits, credits, and UPI payments in the background — categorized by AI instantly.

Intelligent Features

Smart Categories

AI auto-categorizes every expense — food, transport, bills, shopping, health, entertainment — with the ability to learn from your corrections over time.

Spending Insights

Beautiful charts and AI-generated weekly/monthly summaries show where your money goes, highlight unusual spends, and suggest savings opportunities.

Budget Alerts

Set budgets per category and get smart alerts before you overspend. AI learns your patterns and warns you proactively based on spending velocity.

Multi-Currency

Supports multiple currencies with offline conversion. Perfect for travelers who need to track expenses across different countries and currencies.

Export & Share

Export your expense data as CSV or PDF reports. Share monthly summaries with family, accountants, or just keep clean records for tax season.

Complete Privacy

All AI processing — voice recognition, OCR, NLP, and SMS parsing — happens entirely on your device. Your financial data never touches any server.
