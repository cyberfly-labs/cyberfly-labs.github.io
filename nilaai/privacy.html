Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

Nila AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Nila AI is a fully offline application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data, voice recordings, conversations, or learning progress on any external servers.

  • All speech recognition (ASR), text-to-speech (TTS), and language model (LLM) processing happens locally on your device.
  • We do not record, upload, or share your voice data with any third party.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone: To capture your spoken English for real-time speech recognition. Audio is processed on-device and never transmitted.
  • Storage: To store AI models and your learning progress locally.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@cyberflylabs.com.
