Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Overview

Nila AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Nila AI handles your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Nila AI is a fully offline application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data, voice recordings, conversations, or learning progress on any external servers.

  • All speech recognition (ASR) processing happens locally on your device.
  • All text-to-speech (TTS) synthesis happens locally on your device.
  • All language model (LLM) processing happens locally on your device.
  • We do not record, upload, or share your voice data with any third party.
  • Your conversation history and learning progress are stored only on your device.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Microphone: To capture your spoken English for real-time speech recognition and pronunciation feedback. Audio is processed on-device and never transmitted.
  • Storage: To store AI models and your learning progress locally on your device.

4. On-Device AI Models

Nila AI uses on-device AI models (ASR, TTS, and LLM) that run entirely on your phone. These models are downloaded once during initial setup and do not require an internet connection to function afterwards.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since all processing is on-device and no data is transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cyberflylabs.com.
